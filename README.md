# AI for Eastside Rebuild

A curated reading of the *AI for FIs* newsletter, pulled out for the work of
[Eastside Rebuild](https://www.eastsiderebuild.org/), a reuse-and-repair
nonprofit in Cashmere, WA (a salvage warehouse, a 550-tool lending library,
repair cafes, and workshops).

**Read it:** https://septapod.github.io/ai-for-eastside-rebuild/

It was made by pointing Claude at all 19 issues of
[AI for FIs](https://ai4fis.beehiiv.com/) and asking it to keep only what a
reuse warehouse, a tool library, and a repair-cafe crew could use. Every item
links back to its source issue.

One self-contained HTML file, no build step.
